# Pet Customization Manual Acceptance

Automated CI cannot replace real WebGL, color-legibility, and Chrome extension synchronization checks.

## Nose and mouth

- Select all five nose styles and verify clearly different front silhouettes, depth, or materials.
- Select all five mouth styles and distinguish smile, cat, line, open, and pout immediately.
- Play talking, eating, happy, and sneeze motions and confirm mouth animation remains active.
- Change muzzle, nose, mouth, tongue, and cheek colors independently.

## Belly patch

- Restore defaults and confirm an ellipse belly.
- Review all ten silhouettes and verify each label describes its geometry.
- Change color, width, height, horizontal and vertical position, rotation, and softness.
- Check fit on ellipsoid, pear, bean, and rounded-cube bodies.
- Import a legacy `model-default` recipe and confirm migration to ellipse.
- Import legacy custom shield, bean, teardrop, and heart recipes and confirm mapping.

## Colors and ranges

- Change every color channel and confirm immediate preview updates.
- Verify that body/tail-root, limbs/mid-tail, and paws/warm-tail-tip compatibility links are clearly disclosed.
- Move body, head, limbs, ears, eyes, tail, antennae, and belly to the new boundaries.
- Save and reload Studio, confirming every value persists.
- Export and re-import JSON, confirming colors and expanded values remain.
- Values beyond the former recommended range should produce audit guidance rather than silent rollback.

## Interaction and extension

- Drag one slider continuously and confirm one Undo returns to the pre-drag value.
- Verify numeric entry, per-field reset, Undo, and Redo.
- Reload before a formal save and confirm the local draft is restored.
- Choose **Save and use for sync**, then import or synchronize the appearance in Chrome.
- Confirm nose, mouth, belly, and colors match between Studio and the extension.
- Review dark, light, and web backgrounds in all four views.
- Confirm no new permission, upload, or background polling appears.
