# NOVA Browser Agent v0.6.2

## 中文

- 修复“新增规则”和“根据已有请求生成 Mock”进入空白工作区的问题。
- 待机时隐藏持续存在的数据光环。
- 移除转圈、玩耍、玩球和爪尖点点。
- 云朵午睡改为 14 秒平躺姿态。
- 悬停不再打断或延迟补播动作。
- 用户点击动作可经过 240ms 中性过渡后打断当前动作。

## English

- Fixed blank workspaces when creating a rule or generating a Mock from a captured request.
- Hidden the persistent data orbit while idle.
- Removed Spin, Play, Play Ball, and Paw Tap.
- Changed Cloud Nap to a 14-second flat lying pose.
- Hover no longer interrupts or queues delayed motion playback.
- Direct motion selection interrupts the current motion through a 240ms neutral transition.
